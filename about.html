Born from the Nib

Alexandra Sterling Studio began with a single glass pen and an obsession with the tension between wet ink and dry parchment.

What started as a personal meditation grew into a boutique design house sought after by heritage brands and couples who value the deliberate over the immediate. Every flourish we create is a breath held in time.

Begin Your Story
"Writing is a spiritual expression even though it is done by means of a material instrument."

Handwriting is the most intimate form of communication. It carries the weight of the hand, the rhythm of the pulse, and the soul of the sender. At Alexandra Sterling Studio, we are curators of these silent conversations.
